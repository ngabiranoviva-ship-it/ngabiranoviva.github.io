About Us

Youth For Youth Uganda Network was founded to address rising mental health challenges in Africa's youngest population, create We create safe spaces, offer free resources, and conduct outreach in schools and communities.

Our Goals

  • Increase mental health awareness
  • Provide free counseling resources
  • Support the youth through workshops & campaigns
  • Fight stigma around mental health

Services & Resources

  • Peer Counseling: Free youth-to-youth support
  • Mental Health Helplines: Call, SMS, WhatsApp options
  • Downloadable Guides: Stress, depression, anxiety, relationships
  • Workshops: School outreach and youth leadership training
  • Community Groups: Weekly safe-space discussions
